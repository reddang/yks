Goodwood Park Hotel

The Goodwood Park Hotel is one of the finest and most luxurious hotels in Singapore. Her famed Grand Tower has been gazetted as a heritage monument in 1989. Sitting on a 6 hectare piece of land in the heart of Orchard Road, this hotel has survived 2 world wars and has been restored and fitted with modern amenities to be one of the most reputed hospitality landmarks in Singapore with 5 award winning restaurants, 233 guest rooms and luxurious single and double storey suites for long term guests.
